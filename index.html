About Us

New Horizon DXB Hostel is a luxurious hostel located in Jumerah Beach Residence 1, Dubai. The hostel offers a unique and memorable experience for travelers seeking a comfortable stay. The hostel provides top-notch amenities and modern furnishings, catering to both solo adventurers and groups. The prime location offers easy access to Jumerah Beach, trendy cafes, world-class restaurants, and entertainment options. Whether for business or leisure, New Horizon DXB Hostel is committed to making your stay as comfortable and enjoyable as possible.

Key highlights include the prime location, modern rooms with top-notch amenities, easy access to beach, dining, and entertainment options, and friendly, professional staff. Book your stay at New Horizon DXB Hostel today for an unforgettable Dubai adventure.
